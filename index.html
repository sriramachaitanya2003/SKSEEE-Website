Enhance your future with SKS University

SKS Engineering Entrance Examination (SKSEEE) is conducted for admission to undergraduate engineering programmes in SKS group of Institutions. SKSEEE will be conducted between 17 and 23 April 2023 at designated centres across India and abroad. The duration of the examination will be 2 hours and 30 minutes. Candidates can appear only once for SKSEEE. All questions will be Multiple Choice Questions and one mark for right and 0 for the wrong answer. There will be a total of 125 questions divided into the section Maths/Biology (40 questions), Physics (35 questions), Chemistry (35 questions), Aptitude (10 questions), and English (5 questions). The question paper will be in English only. The complete syllabus is available here: Click for Syllabus . The list of test cities is available in the Information Brochure/ direct selection in application form.
